Info/Explanation of Bounds

The QFT translates the known frequency response (templates) and the

desired performance specifications into required conditions on the

phase and magnitude of the nominal loop, L0. These requirements

are referred to as 'bounds.'

Bounds are computed at several frequencies, and are represented

in the Toolbox by two line styles: a solid line designates 'stay above'

and a dashed line designates 'stay below.' That is, L0 must stay above

or below its bound at the frequency of that bound.

The bounds are labeled via colors in the upper left-hand

corner of the figure window in an ascending order by frequency:

r-red, g-green, b-blue, y-yellow, p-purple, c-cyan. Colors are

then repeated if there are more than 6 frequencies.
